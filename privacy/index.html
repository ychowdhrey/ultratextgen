Privacy Policy

How UltraTextGen handles your data, analytics, and cookies.

UltraTextGen respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use this website, how that information is used, and what this website does not do.

UltraTextGen is a browser-based Unicode text generator. The tool runs entirely in your browser. The text you enter into the tool is processed directly in your browser and is not sent to or stored on our servers.

We do not require user accounts. We do not collect personal information through the tool. We do not sell user data.

Effective Date: March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect anonymized usage data to understand how visitors use the website. This is handled through Google Analytics, managed via Google Tag Manager.

Analytics Data
Google Analytics collects aggregated, anonymized information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, traffic sources, and device types. This data does not identify you personally.

Browser Information
Your browser automatically provides technical information to analytics services, such as device type, browser type, operating system, and referring pages. We do not store this information on our own servers.

We do not collect personal identity information. We do not store the text you enter into the generator.

How We Use localStorage

UltraTextGen uses your browser's localStorage to improve your experience. This may include:

  • recently generated text
  • formatting selections
  • interface preferences

This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can remove it at any time by clearing your browser storage.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website uses cookies. Cookies are set by Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager for the following purposes:

  • analytics — to understand how visitors use the site
  • site performance — to measure and improve page load and usability
  • potential advertising functionality — Google Tag Manager may load scripts that support advertising features

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

Google Tag Manager and Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager to manage our analytics infrastructure. Google Tag Manager loads Google Analytics, which collects aggregated usage information such as:

  • pages visited
  • time spent on pages
  • traffic sources
  • device types

Google Tag Manager itself does not store personal information. Google Analytics collects anonymized data to help us understand how the website is used and how to improve it.

Third-Party Services

UltraTextGen uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — used to collect anonymized usage statistics about how visitors interact with the website
  • Google Tag Manager — used to manage and deploy analytics scripts on the website

These services may place cookies or collect anonymized usage information according to their own privacy policies. UltraTextGen does not control how Google handles data collected through these services.

Data Usage

The anonymized analytics data we collect is used to:

  • understand how visitors use the website
  • improve website functionality and performance
  • identify and fix usability issues

We do not sell user data. We do not share personal information with third parties.

External Links

This website may contain links to other websites. Once you leave UltraTextGen, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. Please review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

UltraTextGen does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this website, please contact the site owner so the issue can be reviewed.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the website or legal requirements.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the site owner through the contact information available on the website.
