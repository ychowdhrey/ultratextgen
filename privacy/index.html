Privacy Policy

How UltraTextGen handles your data, analytics, and browser storage.

UltraTextGen respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website, how that information is used, and the choices available to you.

UltraTextGen is designed to work without requiring account creation or direct submission of personal information for normal use.

Effective Date: March 2026

Information We Collect

UltraTextGen collects minimal information required for the tool to function and to understand general website usage.

Types of information that may be collected include:

Browser Information
Your browser may automatically provide technical information such as device type, browser type, operating system, referring pages, and general usage patterns.

Analytics Data
Anonymous usage data may be collected through analytics tools managed via Google Tag Manager. This helps understand how visitors interact with the website.

Local Storage Data
The site may store small pieces of information in your browser using localStorage to improve functionality and user experience.

UltraTextGen does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal identity information.

How We Use localStorage

UltraTextGen may store information in your browser using localStorage. This may include:

  • recently generated text
  • formatting selections
  • interface preferences

This data stays on your device and can be removed at any time by clearing your browser storage.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

UltraTextGen may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and website functionality. These technologies help measure:

  • page visits
  • user interaction
  • website performance

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

Google Tag Manager and Analytics

UltraTextGen may use Google Tag Manager to manage analytics and tracking scripts. These tools may collect aggregated usage information such as:

  • pages visited
  • time spent on pages
  • traffic sources
  • device types

This information helps improve the usability and performance of the website. Google Tag Manager itself does not typically store personal information but may load scripts that collect analytics data.

Third-Party Services

UltraTextGen may rely on third-party services to operate and analyze the website. These may include:

  • analytics providers
  • hosting services
  • content delivery networks (CDNs)
  • advertising services (such as Google AdSense in the future)

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. UltraTextGen does not control how third-party services handle data.

Data Usage

Any collected data may be used for purposes such as:

  • improving website functionality
  • understanding how the tool is used
  • optimizing performance and user experience
  • maintaining security and preventing abuse

UltraTextGen does not sell personal information.

External Links

This website may contain links to other websites. Once you leave UltraTextGen, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. Users should review the privacy policies of any external sites they visit.

Children's Privacy

UltraTextGen is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is believed to have been provided, please contact the site owner so the issue can be reviewed.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the website, analytics tools, or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the site owner through the contact information available on the website.
