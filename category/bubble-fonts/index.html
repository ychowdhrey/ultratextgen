Drawing a bubble letter takes three steps.

Step 1: Lightly pencil the plain letter as a thin skeleton.
Step 2: Draw a rounded, puffy outline about a finger-width around every stroke of the letter.
Step 3: Round off the corners, erase the pencil skeleton, then ink the outline and color it in.

A quick way to learn the shape is to print the outline for a letter from the Bubble Letters A–Z section and trace over it a few times until it feels natural.
