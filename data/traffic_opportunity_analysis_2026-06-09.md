**Date:** 2026-06-09
**Method:** LingoJam page/volume footprint analyzed against UltraTextGen's existing pages, validated with Semrush MCP (US database).
**Competitor benchmark:** `lingojam.com` | **Our domain:** `ultratextgen.com`

---

## Baseline (Semrush, US)

| Metric | LingoJam | UltraTextGen |
|---|---|---|

| #2 / #3 pages | CursiveTextGenerator 105K, FancyLetters 92K | zalgo-text 12, vertical-text 3 |

**Core diagnosis:** UltraTextGen is *not* short on pages — it has 14 category pages, 80+ library pages, 11 platform pages, use-case + guide hubs. The problem is **almost none of them rank on page 1.** Existing pages sit at positions 12–72 (page 2–7) on terms they were built for. So the dominant lever is **improving existing pages to break onto page 1**, complemented by a short list of genuinely missing high-traffic page *types* LingoJam monetizes that we don't have at all.

All search volumes and positions below are pulled live from Semrush.

### Tier 2 — Discord long-tail consolidation (our one proven strength)
`/discord/` already ranks for 66 keywords and is 82% of our traffic. Dozens of discord variants sit at #7–#60 with LJ at #1–#3: *discord name font, discord username font, discord bold font, cool fonts for discord, how to change discord name font, fancy discord fonts,* etc. Tightening this one page (or spinning sub-pages) is the fastest realistic page-1 win because we already have topical traction here.

**Recommended improvement playbook (applies to all above):**
1. Live, instant generator above the fold (LingoJam's whole ranking model is the working tool + copy buttons).
2. Exact-match H1 + title for the head term; FAQ JSON-LD targeting the question variants already in the gap data.
3. Internal links from homepage + related category/library pages (these orphan-ish pages get 0 link equity today).
4. Expand thin category pages with examples, copy-paste blocks, and "X for Instagram/Discord/TikTok" sections.

## Prioritized action sequence

1. **Fix `/discord/` to page 1** — only proven asset; nearest-term traffic. (Improve)
2. **Rebuild `category/cursive-fonts/` as a real generator** — ~390K cluster, biggest single improve lever. (Improve)
3. **Ship a Fancy Text Generator flagship** — biggest create gap, ~163K. (Create)
4. **Low-KD quick wins:** cute-fonts (KD28), small-text (KD22), Old English translator (KD27). Fast page-1 potential. (Mix)
5. **Instagram + Cool Fonts + Bold** clusters next. (Improve + Create)
6. Roll out remaining translators (Wingdings, Hieroglyphics, Pig Latin, Super/Subscript). (Create)

**Underlying constraint:** at ~83 traffic vs LingoJam's ~1.4M, domain authority is the ceiling. Page improvements + new pages must be paired with internal linking and link-building or rankings will stall on page 2 regardless of on-page quality.
