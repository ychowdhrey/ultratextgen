What Font Does YouTube Use?

Short answer first, then details: Roboto for UI text, YouTube Sans for branding, and what creators usually mean when they search "YouTube Sans font."

Quick answer

YouTube uses Roboto for most interface text and YouTube Sans for logo and brand-led type treatments. In other words: Roboto is the product UI workhorse, while YouTube Sans is the branded voice you see in identity-focused surfaces.

Roboto for UI, YouTube Sans for brand

Roboto is optimized for dense UI and metadata readability, which is why it appears across most day-to-day YouTube experiences. YouTube Sans is a brand-forward family used where visual identity needs more personality and distinction.

"YouTube Sans" meme-font confusion

Confusion

"YouTube font" means one thing

  • People use this phrase for UI typography, logo lettering, or creator-added subtitle styles.
Reality

Meme captions are not YouTube Sans

  • Most meme overlays are added in editing tools and use unrelated fonts.
Tip

Separate brand font from styled Unicode text

  • Brand fonts describe YouTube's product identity; Unicode text tools style your copy for profile fields.
