Cute kaomoji (also spelled cute kamoji) are kawaii Japanese-style text faces built from soft, rounded characters — sparkly eyes, little flowers, and blushing cheeks. A cute kaomoji such as (◕‿◕✿) or (ﾉ◕ヮ◕)ﾉ*:･ﾟ✧ is plain Unicode text, so it pastes cleanly into bios, captions, comments, and usernames.
