Media Control Symbols

Play, pause, stop, skip, eject, and power buttons — the full media-transport set. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

Media control symbols are the universal buttons for playback and power. This library collects the play, pause, stop, and record glyphs, the skip, seek, fast-forward, and eject controls, and the IEC power symbols used on switches and devices — all plain Unicode characters you can copy into interfaces, docs, and posts.
