Chinese Symbols

Meaningful Chinese characters — luck, love, dragons, and nature — each labeled with pinyin and meaning. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

Chinese symbols carry centuries of meaning in a single character, which is why hanzi like 福 (good fortune) and 龍 (dragon) appear in usernames, bios, tattoos, and Lunar New Year posts worldwide. This library collects culturally meaningful Chinese characters organized by theme, each labeled with its pinyin romanization and English meaning so you know exactly what you are copying. Use them to add depth to a profile, decorate a caption, or send an auspicious greeting.
