FAQ

Instagram symbols, answered

How do I add symbols to my Instagram bio?

Click any symbol on this page to copy it, then open Instagram → Edit profile → Bio and paste it in. The same works for captions, comments, and your name field. Use dividers and accents (· ✦ ⊹ ┊ ✿) to separate sections or frame a line of text.

Why do some symbols disappear or show as boxes in my Instagram bio?

Two reasons. First, a blank box means the viewer's device has no glyph for that character — pick a more common symbol. Second, Instagram occasionally strips characters it can't validate when you save; if a symbol vanishes, re-paste a single, widely supported character rather than a long combo.

Can I use symbols in my Instagram name or username?

The Name field (the bold line on your profile) accepts most Unicode symbols and styled text, so decorations work there. The @username (handle) is restricted to lowercase letters, numbers, periods, and underscores, so symbols can't be used in the handle itself.
