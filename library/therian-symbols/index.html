Therian Symbols

The theta-delta (θΔ) community mark plus the paw, moon, and animal symbols of therian and alterhuman identity. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

The therian symbol most people search for is the theta-delta — the Greek letters theta (θ) and delta (Δ) combined — adopted by the therianthropy community in 2003 as a shared, respectful identity mark. Therians are people who experience a deep, non-physical identification with an animal, and the wider alterhuman community uses related marks alongside paw prints, moon phases, and animal emoji. This page gathers the theta-delta in its common Unicode forms together with the nature and animal symbols used in TikTok bios, Tumblr blogs, and Discord profiles.
