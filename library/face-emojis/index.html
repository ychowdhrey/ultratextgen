FAQ

Face emojis, answered

What is the kiss emoji and how is it different from the kissing faces?

The blowing-a-kiss emoji 😘 sends a kiss with a little heart and reads as flirty or loving. The plain kissing faces — 😗 (neutral), 😙 (smiling eyes), and 😚 (closed eyes) — are softer and more innocent, good for friends and family. The lipstick mark 💋 stands in for the kiss itself.

What does the smirk emoji 😏 mean?

The smirking face 😏 signals confidence, smugness, sass, or flirty suggestiveness. It is the go-to for a teasing reply, a sly hint, or a self-satisfied "I told you so" moment.

Which emoji is the lip bite emoji?

The biting lip emoji is 🫦. It is widely used to flag attraction, nervousness, or a flirty "oh my" reaction. Some people also pair the smirk 😏 with it to make the meaning clearer.

What does the pleading face 🥺 mean?

The pleading face 🥺 has big watery puppy-dog eyes. People use it to beg cutely, say "please", show that something is adorable, or guilt-trip a friend in a playful way.

Do these face emojis work on iPhone, Android, Instagram, and Discord?

Yes. These are standard Unicode emojis, so they display on iPhone, Android, Instagram, TikTok, Discord, Snapchat, and WhatsApp. Tap any face on this page to copy it, then paste it wherever you type. A few of the newest faces, like the biting lip 🫦, may show as a box on very old devices.
