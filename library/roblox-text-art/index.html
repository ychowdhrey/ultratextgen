Roblox Text Art

Deadpan man-face-style dot art, blocky smiles, and chat emotes sized for Roblox chat. Click any piece to copy it instantly.

Roblox text art is small character art built to survive the Roblox chat box — deadpan man-face-style approximations, blocky smiles, and one-line emotes drawn with braille and block characters. Because Roblox chat has limited length and strict filtering, the best pieces stay compact and use plain Unicode rather than emojis. Copy a complete face below in one click, or build your own from the chat-safe blocks and dot characters in each section. For username glyphs and decorations, see our separate Roblox Symbols page.
