FAQ

Aesthetic symbols, answered

What's the difference between coquette, kawaii, and y2k symbols?

Coquette symbols lean soft-feminine — bows, ribbons, and dainty hearts (🎀 ୨୧ ʚɞ 𐙚). Kawaii symbols are rounder and cuter, leaning baby-pastel and Japanese-inspired (♡ ෆ ◕‿◕). Y2K symbols echo early-2000s cyber and butterfly motifs with sharper, glossier characters (♱ ✮ 🦋 🫧). All three overlap at the edges, but the vibe each evokes is distinct.

Which aesthetic should I pick for my bio?

Pick the aesthetic that matches the rest of your profile. If your photos are pink, soft, and feminine, coquette or kawaii reads cleanly. If they're rural, earthy, or floral, cottagecore fits. If your tone is alt, dark, or moody, goth or witchy land better. For minimal Pinterest-style profiles, whisper symbols (tiny dots and accents) are the quietest option.
