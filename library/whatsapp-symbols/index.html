WhatsApp Symbols

Ticks, hearts, stars, and decorations for WhatsApp statuses, bios, and group names. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

WhatsApp symbols dress up the parts of the app that plain text leaves bare — your About line, status updates, group names, and broadcast messages. This library collects the characters people paste into WhatsApp most: check-mark ticks, hearts in every colour, stars and sparkles, plus dividers and ready-made status decorations. Everything here is standard Unicode, so it renders on both Android and iPhone without extra apps.
