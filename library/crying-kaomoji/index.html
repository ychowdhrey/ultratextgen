Crying & Sad Kaomoji

Copy and paste crying and sad kaomoji — Japanese text faces for tears, sobbing, and low moods. Click any face to copy it instantly.

Crying and sad kaomoji (also spelled kamoji) are Japanese-style text faces that express tears, sobbing, and gloom using streaming-eye characters like ﹏ and ╥. A crying kaomoji such as (╥﹏╥) or (｡•́︿•̀｡) is plain Unicode text, so it pastes cleanly into bios, captions, comments, and messages.
