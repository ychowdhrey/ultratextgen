LinkedIn Post Formatting Symbols

Copy and paste Unicode symbols specifically tested and verified to work in LinkedIn posts, articles, and profiles. Structure your content with bullets, dividers, checkmarks, and professional emoji. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

LinkedIn doesn't support rich text formatting in posts — no bold, italic, or bullet points from a text editor. But Unicode symbols do render correctly, and many creators use them to add structure and visual hierarchy to their posts. These symbols are specifically tested for LinkedIn compatibility across desktop and mobile.
