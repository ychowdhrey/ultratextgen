Brackets, Braces & Parentheses

A complete reference of Unicode bracket types — from standard and mathematical brackets to CJK, decorative, and full-width variants. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

Unicode contains dozens of bracket variants beyond the standard keyboard characters. CJK brackets are popular for aesthetic bios and usernames, mathematical brackets appear in formal notation, and decorative brackets add visual flair to styled text. This collection organizes all bracket types for quick browsing and copying.
