Traffic & Road Sign Symbols

Unicode symbols and emojis related to traffic, road signs, and transportation infrastructure. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

Actual road signs are images, not Unicode characters. However, Unicode and emoji include a collection of traffic-related symbols that convey similar meaning — traffic lights, prohibition circles, pedestrian symbols, and more. This library collects the best available traffic and road-sign-adjacent characters.
