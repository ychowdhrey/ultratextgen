FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the moon phase symbols in Unicode?
Unicode includes a full set of moon phase emoji: 🌑 (new moon) through 🌘 (waning crescent), plus 🌙 (crescent), 🌚 🌛 🌜 (faces). Text-art crescents include ☽ ☾ ☪. These work on all modern platforms.
What does ☾ mean?
☾ is the Unicode crescent moon symbol (U+263E), representing the waning crescent or left-facing moon. It's distinct from ☽ (right-facing, waxing crescent). Both are popular in witchy, celestial, and aesthetic bios.
Are celestial symbols used in witchy aesthetics too?
Yes. Moon phases and crescent moons are central to both celestial aesthetic and witchy/occult aesthetics. See our Witchy & Occult Symbols library for pentagrams, planetary symbols, and alchemical signs that complement these celestial characters.
Do moon phase symbols work on TikTok and Instagram?
Yes. All moon phase emoji (🌑–🌘 🌙) are universal emoji supported on all platforms. Unicode text symbols like ☾ ☽ ⋆ also copy and paste reliably on Instagram, TikTok, X, Discord, and Pinterest.
How do I write the full moon phase sequence?
Copy the moon phases in order: 🌑🌒🌓🌔🌕🌖🌗🌘. You can add spaces between them — 🌑 🌒 🌓 🌔 🌕 🌖 🌗 🌘 — for a more spaced-out look. This sequence is popular in witchy and celestial aesthetic bios.
