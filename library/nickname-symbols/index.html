Nickname Symbols

Wings, brackets, crowns, and flair characters for stylish gaming nicks — plus ready-made name wrappers. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

Nickname symbols are the decorative Unicode characters players wrap around their in-game names — the ꧁꧂ wings, ツ smiley, 彡 motion lines, and ♛ crowns you see in Free Fire, Mobile Legends, PUBG, and Fortnite lobbies. Because they are ordinary Unicode characters, they work in almost any game or app that accepts text names. Copy individual symbols below, or grab a complete ready-made nick decoration and replace 'YourName' with your own.
