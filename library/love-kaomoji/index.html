Love & Heart Kaomoji

Copy and paste love and heart kaomoji — cute Japanese text faces for crushes, kisses, and hugs. Click any face to copy it instantly.

Love and heart kaomoji (also spelled kamoji) are Japanese-style text faces that show affection using hearts, blushing cheeks, and outstretched arms. Unlike a plain heart emoji, a heart kaomoji such as (♥ω♥) or (づ｡◕‿‿◕｡)づ pastes as pure Unicode text into any bio, caption, comment, or message — so it looks the same everywhere.
