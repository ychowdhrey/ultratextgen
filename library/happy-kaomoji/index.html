Happy Kaomoji

Copy and paste happy kaomoji — smiling, excited, and cheerful Japanese text faces. Click any face to copy it instantly.

Happy kaomoji (also spelled kamoji) are Japanese-style text faces that capture every shade of good mood — a quiet smile, pure excitement, or a celebratory dance. A happy kaomoji such as (◠‿◠) or ＼(≧▽≦)／ is plain Unicode text, so it pastes cleanly into bios, captions, comments, and usernames without breaking.
