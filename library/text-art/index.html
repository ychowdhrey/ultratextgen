Text Art

Finished ASCII and Unicode art pieces plus every box-drawing, block, and braille character you need to make your own. Click to copy instantly.

Text art (also called ASCII art) is artwork drawn entirely with characters — box-drawing lines, shaded blocks, braille dots, and plain keyboard symbols. It works anywhere text does: Discord chats, game lobbies, X posts, YouTube comments, and plain-text bios. This page collects small, ready-to-paste text art pieces you can copy in one click, plus the individual building-block characters if you want to draw something original.
