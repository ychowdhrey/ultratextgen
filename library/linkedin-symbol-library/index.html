LinkedIn Symbol Library

Professional Unicode symbols curated for LinkedIn posts, headlines, and comments. Click any symbol to copy it to your clipboard.

LinkedIn rewards clarity and visual structure. The right symbol — an arrow for progression, a numbered marker for frameworks, a clean bullet for lists — turns a dense paragraph into scannable, professional content that earns engagement.

Every symbol below is tested across LinkedIn's desktop and mobile rendering. Click once to copy, then paste directly into your post, comment, or LinkedIn headline. For techniques on styling comments with Unicode formatting, see the LinkedIn Comment Styling guide. To master the strategic side of commenting, explore the LinkedIn Comment Archetypes guide.
