Recycle & Environment Symbols

The universal recycling sign, plastic resin codes, and eco-friendly glyphs in one place. Click any symbol to copy it instantly.

The recycling arrows are among the most recognizable symbols in the world, and Unicode encodes a full set of them. This library gathers the universal recycling signs, the numbered plastic resin-identification codes (♳ through ♹) used on packaging, and the green eco and environment glyphs for sustainability content. Click any character to copy it for labels, posts, or product descriptions.
