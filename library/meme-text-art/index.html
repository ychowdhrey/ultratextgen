Meme Text Art

Reaction faces, lenny packs, table flips, and dot art memes built from braille and block characters. Click any piece to copy it instantly.

Meme text art is the reaction-face side of ASCII art: lenny faces, table flips, disapproving stares, and braille dot art faces you paste into Discord, Twitch, and game chats for comedic effect. Unlike decorative text art, these pieces exist to be dropped into a conversation at exactly the right moment. Copy a complete face or flip set below in one click, or grab the shading blocks, braille density characters, and face-building glyphs to assemble your own meme.
