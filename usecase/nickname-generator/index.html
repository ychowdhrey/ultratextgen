How to Make a Stylish Nickname

Start with a base style that matches the vibe you want: cursive and script read as soft and aesthetic, gothic feels dark and edgy, and small caps stays clean and readable at tiny sizes. Type your name above and click the style you like to copy it.

Then frame it. For a gamer look, wrap the name in ꧁꧂ or ༺༻ and add a slash 彡 or dagger †. For a cute look, use ꒰ ꒱ brackets with sparkles ⋆ ✧ and a heart ♡. Keep symbols to one or two on each side so the name stays readable.

If a platform rejects your nickname or shows boxes, the style isn't supported there — switch to Small Caps or Bold, which have the widest support across games and apps.
