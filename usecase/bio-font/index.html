Platform Compatibility & Limits

Discord's "About Me" supports up to 190 characters of full Unicode, including styled fonts (cursive, gothic, bold), symbols and emoji. The same Unicode works in usernames, server nicknames and channel names. A handful of rare characters may show as boxes on very old Android devices, but the common styles render everywhere. Select Discord in the platform picker above to see the 190-character limit live as you type.
Platforms enforce character limits:

Instagram: 150 characters for bio, 30 characters for username (only letters, numbers, periods, underscores)
TikTok: 80 characters for bio
Discord: 190 characters for "About Me"
X (Twitter): 160 characters for bio
Tinder: 500 characters for "About me"

Our platform picker shows the right limit live as you type — trim if the counter turns red.

Instagram & TikTok usually allow emojis and many symbols in bios and usernames.
Discord supports a wide range but may limit very complex combinations.
Some older games or strict platforms block certain Unicode characters.

Always test by pasting into the bio or username field before saving.
