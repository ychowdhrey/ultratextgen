Those comments use Unicode characters that visually resemble bold or italic versions of the Latin alphabet. They aren't formatted text — they're entirely different characters that happen to look styled. Our comment font generator creates those characters instantly from any text you type.

Choosing the Right Style for Your Comment

Match the font to the intent behind your comment:

Making a point or correcting someone? → Bold (𝗟𝗶𝗸𝗲 𝘁𝗵𝗶𝘀) signals authority
Being sarcastic or witty? → Strikethrough (t̶o̶t̶a̶l̶l̶y̶ ̶g̶r̶e̶a̶t̶) adds irony without extra words
Giving a sincere compliment? → Script / Cursive (𝒯𝒽𝒾𝓈 𝓂𝒶𝒹𝑒 𝓂𝓎 𝒹𝒶𝓎) feels warm and personal
Celebrating or hyping someone? → Bubble text (Ⓛⓔⓖⓔⓝⓓⓐⓡⓨ) is fun and lighthearted
Setting a vibe (music, gaming, alt)? → Gothic / Fraktur (𝔗𝔥𝔦𝔰 𝔱𝔯𝔞𝔠𝔨 𝔥𝔦𝔱𝔰 𝔡𝔦𝔣𝔣𝔢𝔯𝔢𝔫𝔱) carries aesthetic weight
Being measured or thoughtful? → Double-struck (𝕋𝕙𝕖 𝕕𝕒𝕥𝕒 𝕤𝕒𝕪𝕤 𝕠𝕥𝕙𝕖𝕣𝕨𝕚𝕤𝕖) sounds precise
