How to Make a Clan Tag the Whole Squad Shares

Start with the tag: 2–5 letters, ideally a word or initials your team already says out loud. Type it above and pick a font — Bold and Small Caps read cleanest and have the widest game support, while Gothic and cursive look sharper but may not render everywhere.

Then frame it. Square brackets [TAG] read as classic esports; ꧁꧂ and ★彡 go for a flashier look. Keep it to one symbol on each side so the tag fits the game's character limit and doesn't get cut off in the kill feed.

Finally, hit Share Team Template. Drop the link in your clan's Discord — each member opens it, types their own name, and the identical font and frame are applied automatically. If a tag shows as boxes (▯) or gets rejected, switch to Bold or Small Caps, which pass the most game filters.
