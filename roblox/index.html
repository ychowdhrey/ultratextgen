Quick reference

@Username vs Display Name

Your @username is your unique login ID — costs 1,000 Robux to change, ASCII-only, 3–20 chars. Your display name is what players see in-game — free once every 7 days, supports Unicode symbols, not unique. Fancy symbols go in the display name; the generator above targets the @username field.
